Before you continue you are required to prove who you are

You will need:

  • UK passport (a passport that exprired with in the last 12 months can be used)

You will need one of the following if you do not have a UK passport:

  • non UK-passport
  • biometric residence card (BRC)
  • biometric residence permit (BRP)

You may need:

  • national insurance number
  • GB driving or provisional driving licence number
