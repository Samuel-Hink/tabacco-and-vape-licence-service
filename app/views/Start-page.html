Get your tobacco and vapping licence. To apply you must:

  • Be over the age of 18
  • Be able to provide valid proof of age to demonstrate that you meet the legal age requirement
  • Complete and submit a license application form provided

Applying takes around 5 minutes.

Before you start

You’ll need:

an identity document such as your passport or driving licence

an address where you’ve live for up to three years

complete and submit a license application form provided

You might be asked for additional information, such as your National Insurance number if you know it.

You’ll get a confirmation email after you’ve applied.

Your licence should arrive within one week if you apply online. It may take longer if we need to make additional checks.

You cannot register for this service if you’re in the UK illegally.

Cost

It’s free to apply to get a tobacco and vaping licence. However, you will need to meet all set our criteria before applying.

You cannot register for this service if you’re in the UK illegally.

The online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg)

If you already have a licence

  • renew your licence
  • replace your licence if it is lost, stolen, damaged or destroyed
  • change your details such as name, address, and contact details in Subsection
